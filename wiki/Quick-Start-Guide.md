## Common First-Time Questions

### "Can I edit the AI workout?"
Not directly during generation, but you can:
- Save as template and edit
- Manually log a custom workout
- Regenerate if you don't like it

### "What if I can't complete a set?"
- Log what you actually did (e.g., 6 reps instead of 8)
- AI will adjust future workouts accordingly
- No penalties - be honest!

### "How does progressive overload work?"
- AI tracks your last performance for each exercise
- Suggests +2.5-5lbs when you hit target reps
- Or +1-2 reps if you can't increase weight
- Gradual, safe progression

### "Can I skip exercises?"
- Yes! Just don't mark them complete
- Or delete from template if saving
- AI learns your preferences

### "What if I work out at a different gym?"
- Use Manual Workout Log
- Record what you did
- Keeps your history complete
- AI still factors it in
